How to Play
Objective:

Match all the pairs of cards on the grid before time runs out.

How to Play:
  • Click any two cards to flip them over.
  • If the cards match, they stay flipped. If they don't match, they flip back after a short time.
  • Continue matching pairs until all cards are matched to complete the level.
Levels:

The game starts at Level 1 with 8 pairs of cards. As you progress, more pairs are added, and the time is reduced.

Winning & Losing:
  • If you match all pairs within the time limit, you win the level and move to the next one.
  • If time runs out, you lose, but you can retry the level.
